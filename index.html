About MealPlanner

Welcome to MealPlanner, your ultimate companion for discovering delicious recipes and organizing your weekly meals. We believe that meal planning should be simple, enjoyable, and inspiring.

Our platform uses TheMealDB API to bring you thousands of recipes from cuisines around the world. Whether you're looking for quick weeknight dinners, elaborate weekend projects, or dietary-specific meals, we've got you covered.
